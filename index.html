Step 4: Get Your Data Key

Your store is live! Now, grab the connection string for the AI:

  1. Go to your Vercel Dashboard.
  2. Click on your new project (e.g. pm-lab-...).
  3. In the top menu, click the Storage tab.
  4. Select Neon and copy the Postgres Connection String (starts with postgres://).

Step 5: Connect Claude

Open Claude Desktop > Settings > MCP > Edit Config. Paste this block:

"postgresql": { "command": "npx", "args": [ "-y", "@modelcontextprotocol/server-postgres", "PASTE_URL_HERE?sslmode=require" ] }

Final Task: Replace 'PASTE_URL_HERE' with your unique key and save the file.
