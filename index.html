Step 3: Database Infrastructure

Your lab requires a Postgres database to store product and order data.

Action Required:
  • Vercel Dashboard > Add New... > Storage > Neon > Create.
  • Press Continue. Name it: ...
  • Press Create, then press Done.
  • Go to the Quickstart tab > .env.local section.
  • Click Show secret (eye icon) and copy the DATABASE_URL.
