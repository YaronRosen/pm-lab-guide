Step 1: Lab Identity

Enter the details for your course assignment.

Step 2: Create Your Database

You need a database to store product and user data.

  1. Go to Vercel Dashboard.
  2. Click the Storage tab.
  3. Click Create Database & select Neon (PostgreSQL).
  4. Once created, find DATABASE_URL, click the "eye" to reveal it, and copy the full link.

Step 3: Launch Lab

* Deployment will take 3-5 minutes. Follow the logs in the next screen.
