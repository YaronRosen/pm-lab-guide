Database Infrastructure

Your store needs a Postgres Database to save product and customer data. We will provision this during the launch.

The "No-Fail" Secret: In the next step, once the Vercel screen opens, look for the Storage section immediately and click Connect Neon Postgres before doing anything else.

Deploy Your Environment

Click the button below to trigger the deployment. Follow the prompts on the Vercel screen.

1. Storage: Click "Connect Neon Postgres" first.
2. Variable: Enter nextjs for the onboarding type.
3. Action: Click the Deploy button.

Access Connection String

Now that your infrastructure is provisioned, let's grab the access key for your AI Analyst.

  1. Open your Vercel Dashboard and select the new project.
  2. Navigate to Storage > Neon.
  3. Copy the Postgres Connection String.

Connect Claude AI

Open Claude Desktop (Settings > MCP > Edit Config) and paste this block to link the AI to your database.

"postgresql": { "command": "npx", "args": ["-y", "@modelcontextprotocol/server-postgres", "PASTE_URL_HERE?sslmode=require"] }

System Verification

Let's confirm the connection is active. Open a new chat in Claude and ask:

"Connect to my database and tell me the names of the top 3 products in stock."

Setup Complete
